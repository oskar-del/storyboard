The Problem

Every AI session
starts with amnesia.

The AI forgets everything. You re-explain, re-decide, re-discover — on repeat. This isn't a context window problem. It's a memory problem.

"Here's what we're building, here's the stack, here's where we left off…"
🔁 Re-explained every session
"Did we already try this? I can't remember if we ruled it out last week."
🌫️ Decisions that evaporate
"The context window filled up again. Starting a new chat. Back to zero."
⏱️ Context resets kill momentum
The Solution

One canvas.
Every AI tool.
Zero re-explaining.

Storyboard sits above every AI tool you use. It captures the work, connects the decisions, and seeds perfect context into any new chat — in one click.

1

Capture

Sessions, decisions, ideas — logged automatically via MCP or with one click in the browser extension. Works across Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, Cursor.

2

Canvas

All your projects on one visual board. Filter by time, type, project. Search everything with ⌘K. Skills library with health scores and intent statements.

3

Seed

One click generates a complete context brief — north star, recent sessions, key decisions, open ideas. Paste into any AI. Instant full context.
