---

## The journey

**"Founder from zero"** — for someone starting a business with AI from scratch.

Not a power user. Not an enterprise buyer. Someone who woke up with an idea and wants to move fast without a team.

What they need on day one:
- Where do I register the company?
- How do I set up Gmail, analytics, social accounts?
- What can AI handle for me right now vs. what do I do manually?
- What's my plan for today?

What Storyboard gives them:
- A structured project from session 1 — every decision captured, every step logged
- A daily briefing: *"Here's where you were, here's what's next, here's what you automated yesterday"*
- A seed that travels: when they open a new AI session, Storyboard seeds it with full context — the Charles Protocol "Genesis prompt" pattern, but auto-generated from their actual project history

The morning outline is the hook. Every morning: here's your three things for today, here's one thing AI can take off your plate, here's one decision you made last week that's relevant right now.

---

## Why this is different from the other journeys

The current journeys (Business audit / AI operator / Claude power user / Enterprise) are all about people who already operate with AI and want to do it better.

"Founder from zero" is the earliest possible entry point. These people are just starting. They don't have a Storyboard yet — Storyboard *is* their operating system from day one. That's a different user and a different value prop. The retention model is different too: they're not switching from anything, they're growing into it.

---

## Connection to Charles Protocol

Charles Protocol is the full product in this direction — AI agents handling operations, blockchain coordination, tokenization infrastructure. That's a separate venture and a separate build.

What Storyboard could borrow: the **Genesis prompt pattern**. A structured session-start template for founders that seeds the AI with:
- Who you are
- What you're building
- What you decided last session
- What needs to happen today

That's not a full Charles Protocol build. It's one feature: a smarter seed generator for the "Founder" journey.

---

## What would need to exist to build this

- A "Founder" project template in Storyboard (pre-loaded blocks: company setup, first customers, first automation)
- A Genesis prompt generator: takes your blocks → produces a structured "start of day" seed
- A daily briefing block type: auto-generated summary of what's next, what's stale, what needs a decision
- Journey onboarding: when you pick "Founder from zero," Storyboard walks you through the first 7 days

---

## Pitch framing

*"Most tools help experienced operators move faster. Storyboard is the one that builds with you from day one — so by the time you're an operator, you already have the memory, the decisions, and the context to prove it."*

## Not building now

Capture only. This belongs on the journey personas roadmap, probably after the core context/provenance features are solid. The value of this journey is highest when Storyboard already has robust seeding — so the "Genesis prompt" a founder gets on day 90 is genuinely powered by 90 days of captured decisions.
