How It Works

Three steps to full clarity

From zero context to complete project memory in minutes.

1

Connect your projects

Point Storyboard at your working folders. It reads your sessions, git history, and existing files to build a retroactive storyboard — even for work you did months ago.

2

Capture what matters

As you work, Storyboard captures session blocks automatically. Add decisions and ideas with the FAB button or from the dashboard. Everything is timestamped and searchable.

3

Seed any new chat

Hit "Seed New Chat" and a complete digest — north star, recent sessions, open decisions, key context — is ready to paste into any AI window. Instant full context, every time.
